Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026 · Draft — review before launch

What we collect

  • Account details — your email address, used to give you access to the planner and send product updates during the founding period.
  • Planner data — the tasks and plans you create, stored so the planner works across your devices.
  • Anonymous usage — which buttons on the landing page are clicked, so we can understand what visitors find useful. No personal details are attached.
  • Payments — handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties beyond the services needed to run the product (hosting, database and payments).

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Use the contact form and we will respond promptly.
