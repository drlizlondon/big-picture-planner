The missing step

Most planning tools split your life in two.

Your calendar has the fixed things — meetings, school runs, appointments and deadlines.

Your to-do list has everything else — errands, admin, chores, calls, returns, forms, ideas and loose ends.

But the real question is not “what do I need to do?”

It is “where does this actually fit?”

Big Picture Planner is built for that missing step.

How it works

Three steps from chaotic week to visual plan.

1

Dump it all into your Life Inbox

Capture every floating task before it clutters your head. School forms, dentist bookings, that call you keep putting off — everything goes in, with no date needed yet.

2

See the real shape of your week

View your fixed commitments and open gaps together. The gaps are where your week actually happens — and now you can see them.

3

Drag tasks into the spaces where they fit

Build a realistic week by placing tasks visually, instead of hoping a list will somehow turn into a plan.
