How it works

Three steps from overwhelmed to sorted.

1

Dump everything into your Life Inbox

School trip permission slip. Dentist. That report that's due Friday. That call you keep forgetting. Everything goes in here — no time, no date, just a list of things that need a home.

2

See your whole week at a glance

Your fixed commitments are already there. School pickup at 3pm. That meeting Wednesday. Now you can actually see the gaps — the real, usable gaps — where things can fit.

3

Drag each task into the gap where it fits

Pick up a task from your inbox. Find the slot. Drop it in. Like Tetris — except when the pieces fit, your week actually works. Move things around until it all makes sense.
