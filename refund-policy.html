The 14-day beta guarantee

Founding Access comes with a 14-day beta guarantee. Try Big Picture Planner properly for 14 days from the date of purchase. If you use it and feel it does not help you plan your week more clearly, email us and we will refund your payment.

How it works

  • Request your refund within 14 days of purchase, via the contact details below.

What we ask in return

The guarantee is designed for genuine early users. We ask that you actually open the planner and try planning a week with it before requesting a refund. We may decline requests where the account shows the product was never used.

This policy is in addition to your statutory rights under UK consumer law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013. Nothing here affects those rights.
