How it works

Two things, working together.

1

Add everything to your to-do list

Your Life Inbox is your to-do list. Add every task that needs to happen this week — no date, no time, no decisions yet. Food shop, that email, the appointment, the thing you keep forgetting. Just get it all down.

✓ Out of your head, into one place
2

See the whole week in the calendar

The week view shows everything: your fixed commitments, your Google Calendar events, and the real gaps where tasks can actually fit. Not imaginary free time — real slots, on real days.

✓ See what the week can actually hold
3

Drag tasks from your list into the calendar

Pick a task from your to-do list, find the gap in the calendar, drop it in. Move things around until the week makes sense. Like Tetris — except when the pieces fit, everything actually gets done.

✓ Satisfying, concrete, done
